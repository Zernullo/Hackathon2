Financial Awareness

Understanding Taxes

Taxes are mandatory contributions levied on individuals or corporations by a government entity. Understanding how taxes work is crucial for financial planning and compliance.

Types of Taxes

  • Income Tax: Tax on individual or corporate earnings.
  • Sales Tax: Tax on sales of goods and services.
  • Property Tax: Tax on property ownership.
  • Capital Gains Tax: Tax on the profit from the sale of assets or investments.

Tax Deductions and Credits

Tax deductions and credits can significantly reduce your tax liability. Common deductions include mortgage interest, charitable contributions, and medical expenses. Tax credits, such as the Earned Income Tax Credit (EITC), directly reduce the amount of tax you owe.

Tips for Tax Planning

  • Keep accurate records of your income and expenses.
  • Understand the tax implications of your investments.
  • Take advantage of tax-advantaged accounts like IRAs and 401(k)s.
  • Consult with a tax professional for personalized advice.
